About Hail Seitan

Hail Seitan has been proudly serving vegan food for over 10 years, here in NomNom, Texas. Our menu ranges from traditional southern dishes and barbeque top to italian food, and everything in between. We have a roatating menu full of amazing food that does not use any animal products at all. We also give 10% of our profits to the North Texas Humane Society. Please bring your friends and family, and enjoy a meal that won't weigh on your conscience.
